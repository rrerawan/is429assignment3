About

Lunch2U is a one stop solution for inconvenience of having to battle the lunchtime crowd. We offer a meal subscription service where you can request for a meal and we bring it right to you. That means that you no longer have to battle the lunch crowd every day!

Delivery

We deliver your meals right up to your doorstep, ensuring that you get your meals hot and fresh, every single day!

Customization

Pick from one of the many cuisines that we offer everyday. From Chinese to Italian to Indian, we're sure to satisfy your cravings!

Surprise

Can't decide what to have for the day? Pick the 'Surprise Me' option for something unexpectedly delightful!

How Lunch2U works

  • Select a subscription plan

    Pick a plan that best suits you and subscribe! You will be able to start placing your orders just 3 days after your subscription has been confirmed!

  • Tell us your dietary preferences

    Picky about your food? Let us know what you love and what you don't, and we will ensure that the food you receive will be to your taste.

  • Request food delivery

    Let us know when you want food delivered to you by logging in and selecting the cuisine you would like to have for the day. If you're feeling adventurous, you could alternatively select the surprise option.

  • Collect & Enjoy your meal

    Once you have made your selection, just sit back and relax while we bring your meal to you. Enjoy!
